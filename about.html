About Us

01. Company

Projects Bucket is a coalition between individual intellectuals coming from various backgrounds and experiences, but united by the goal of helping College Students make an impact at their college and interviews.

The Mission

The Vision

As we, all the developers, have come through the same path of college, we faced the same problems that you are facing now and we have a clear understanding of what to do to over come this.

The team consists of talented individuals who are experts in their own fields and have years of experience developing medium to large scale projects for various necessities and needs.
And now, we intend to apply our expertise to the college sector.
The domains we deal with are wide ranging from Artificial Intelligence to Full Stack Web Development to Internet of things applications.
Having an experience of working in the corporate environments, all out developers maintain the required quality and build standards, with absolutely no disregard for both.
