Initial Approach

Use the various forms present and provide a brief description of your requirements. A representative from our side will get in touch with you

Further information can be obtained from the Companies Register of the Slovak Republic.

Deciding the project type

We provide two approaches to developing projects

1.You could provide us an idea and we would build the project according to your requirements and needs

2.Two - It could be an entire project with everything from our side

Gathering Requirements

If you wish to implement your idea through us, our representative will reach out to you and gather all the requirements.

If it is an idea from our side, we will approach you with the idea for an approval.

Building

This phase is entirely on us, you will be approached only when there are tremendous changes required.

Handover and Overview

Here we handover the finished projects to you and provide an orientation session, to help you understand how the components of it work

Support and Assistance

From here on the phase of building code and components end and the phase of building trust starts.

We support and stay behind you to ensure that you could utilize the project to it's full extent and make an impact, as befits you.
